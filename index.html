Interested in a Table Tennis Lesson?

Do you enjoy playing ping pong and want to grow your skills? Having played ping pong competitively for 8+ years, I have encountered hundreds of people who are as enthusiastic about ping pong as I am. There are those who enjoy playing for a good exercise. These are those who play because their family members, friends, or even co-workers play. There are even those who want to dominate in the sport and compete in official tournaments. 

Hi, my name is Tim and I want to be your ping pong trainer. I want to help those of all ages who are at a BEGINNER level because of three reasons:

1. I want to give you an opportunity to get better in the sport
Many people play ping pong, but do not realize there are individuals like myself who can help enhance their skills.

2. I want you to live a better and happier life
Ping pong is a fantastic sport for the body and mind! 

3. I want to grow the sport
Ping Pong is growing more and more popular. Let's keep that going! 

My Coaching Fees:
$35.00 per hour 
$50.00 per 1 1/2 hour

I am an independent trainer. This means I can come to you. I know many facilities where we could train or I could come to your home. However, I charge an extra $10.00 for in house coaching. My rating is 1850 (above average). I strongly believe I am the right fit for new players, beginners, or those with some experience. If you are an intermediate player, an advance player, or if I am not right for you, then I recommend you to look at table tennis centers/clubs in your area.
